I'm Mike

My name is Mike Schmidt. My journey to becoming a web developer started in 2020 when the pandemic hit. I am currently a law enforcement officer who was working in a Denver suburb but I more recently moved to the place I have always considered home; Gunnison, Colorado. While working as a law enforcement officer, I realized I wanted more for myself so my beautiful wife, Anna, suggested that we should start learning how to code. I started listening to some podcasts and got inspired from listening to people who made a career change without needing to go back to college. I reached out to a friend of mine who is a front end developer and he suggested I start my reading Murach's "HTML 5 and CSS 3" to get a basic understanding of building an HTML website. I read through that and later read Jon Duckett’s "HTML & CSS" and "JavaScript & JQuery." Once I became more familiar with JavaScript I purchased O'Reilley's "JavaScript The Definitive Guide 7th Edition" for supplemental reading on the subject.

I then delved into courses on Udemy. I started with Rob Percival’s “The Complete Web Developer Course 2.0” to learn basic HTML, CSS, and Javascript. This course was a bit outdated so I then moved to Dr. Angela Yu’s “The Complete 2021 Web Development Bootcamp” and followed the lessons through the front-end developer course. I was absolutely loving everything I was learning about web development and I wanted to dive deeper into everything JavaScript was capable of, so I then purchased Jonas Schmedtmann’s “The Complete JavaScript Course 2021: From Zero to Expert!”

I spent countless hours during my nights and weekends learning web development, but I also discovered I love doing it. I love improving my code and making my websites even faster and more responsive.
